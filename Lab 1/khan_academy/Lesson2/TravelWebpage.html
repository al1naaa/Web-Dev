Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is a group of three waterfalls at the southern end of Niagara Gorge, spanning the border between the province of Ontario in Canada and the state of New York in the United States.

Top 3 Interesting Facts About Niagara Falls:

  1. World’s fastest-moving waterfall with Highest Flow Rate
  2. Five Tightrope Walks Over Niagara Falls
  3. The first daredevil to survive a trip down the falls was a woman
